Buying vs Leasing a Vehicle

Buy
Pros
Own the vehicle.
No more payments after the vehicle is paid for
Possible longer time periods in between looking for another vehicle
Cons
Usually higher monthly payments for the same vehicle vs leasing
Usually make payments over longer period of time for one vehicle comapred to leasing
May still have to look for another vehicle as soon as or even before you are done paying off this vehicle

Lease
Pros
New vehicle every three years (If you don't mind having a monthly payment)
Monthly payments are often considerably lower compared to buying the same vehicle
Dealerships usually offer great deals to repeat customers
Cons
Always have a monthly payment
Do not own the vehicle
Have to stay tied in to the same dealership to get the repeat customer perks
Could be costly if you decide to stop leasing
